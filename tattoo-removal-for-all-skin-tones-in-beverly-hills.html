How many sessions will I need to completely remove my tattoo?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with treatments spaced 8-10 weeks apart. Professional tattoos typically need 8-12 sessions, while amateur tattoos may clear in 4-6 sessions. Factors affecting the number of sessions include: ink colors (black and red clear faster than blues and greens), tattoo age, ink depth, and your skin tone. During your consultation at our Beverly Hills clinic, we'll assess your specific tattoo and provide a more accurate timeline based on these factors. We use the advanced PicoSure laser system, which often requires fewer sessions than older laser technologies.
Does tattoo removal hurt and what's the recovery process like?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against their skin. We use a medical-grade numbing cream 30 minutes before treatment to minimize discomfort. After treatment, you can expect some redness, swelling, and sensitivity for 3-7 days. The treated area may develop small blisters, which is normal. We provide detailed aftercare instructions, including using ice packs, keeping the area clean, and avoiding sun exposure. Most clients return to work immediately after treatment, though we recommend avoiding strenuous exercise for 48 hours.
