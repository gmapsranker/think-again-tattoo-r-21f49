Frequently Asked Questions About Tattoo Removal Clinic

How much does tattoo removal cost at your Burbank clinic?
The cost varies based on your tattoo's size, color, and density. At Think Again Tattoo Removal, small tattoos (up to 2x2 inches) typically start at $200 per session, medium tattoos (2x2 to 4x4 inches) range from $300-400, and larger pieces are priced during consultation. We offer package pricing with 15% savings when you pre-pay for 3 or more sessions. We also provide free consultations where we can give you an exact quote after examining your tattoo in person.
How many sessions will I need to completely remove my tattoo?
Most clients need 6-12 sessions for complete removal, spaced 8-10 weeks apart. During your consultation at our Burbank clinic, we'll assess factors like ink colors, tattoo age, skin type, and location to give you a more precise estimate. Black and gray tattoos typically require fewer sessions than colorful ones. We use the advanced PicoSure laser system, which often achieves results in fewer sessions than traditional Q-switched lasers.
Does tattoo removal hurt and what's the recovery like?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against their skin. We use medical-grade numbing cream and cooling systems to minimize discomfort. After treatment, you can expect the area to feel like a mild sunburn for 2-3 days. The Southern California heat can affect healing, so we recommend keeping the area covered when outdoors and using our provided aftercare products. Most clients return to normal activities immediately, though you should avoid swimming and direct sun exposure for 1 week.
Are your technicians licensed and certified in California?
Yes, all our laser technicians are certified by the State of California and have completed advanced training in PicoSure laser technology. Our lead technician has over 8 years of experience in laser tattoo removal and holds additional certifications in skin typing and laser safety. We maintain all required Los Angeles County health permits and undergo regular inspections to ensure we meet the highest safety standards.
What if my tattoo doesn't fade as expected?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee at Think Again Tattoo Removal. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed treatment plan with expected results. If your tattoo isn't responding as projected after 3 sessions, we'll adjust your treatment plan at no additional cost. We document progress with high-resolution photos at each session and can modify laser settings or treatment intervals as needed. In the rare case where removal proves unusually difficult, we'll work with you to find a solution, including potential session credits.
