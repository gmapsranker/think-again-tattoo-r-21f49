How long does the removal process take and how many sessions will I need?
Most clients require 6-12 sessions spaced 8-10 weeks apart for complete removal. Factors affecting the timeline include: tattoo age, ink colors (blue and green typically take longer), ink density, and your skin type. During your consultation at our West Hollywood clinic, we'll assess your tattoo and provide a customized treatment plan. While complete removal can take 12-18 months, you'll see progressive fading after each session. We use the advanced PicoSure laser system which often requires fewer sessions than traditional Q-switched lasers.
Does tattoo removal hurt and what's the recovery like?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against the skin. We use a medical-grade cooling system and can apply numbing cream 30 minutes before treatment to minimize discomfort. After treatment, the area may feel like a mild sunburn for 2-3 days. You can return to normal activities immediately, but should avoid sun exposure, swimming, and intense exercise for 48 hours. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and our 24/7 nurse hotline is available if you have concerns during recovery.
