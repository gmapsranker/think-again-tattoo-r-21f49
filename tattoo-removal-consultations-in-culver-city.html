How many sessions will I need to completely remove my tattoo?
Most clients need 6-12 sessions for complete removal, scheduled 8-10 weeks apart to allow proper healing. Black and gray tattoos typically require fewer sessions than colorful ones. During your consultation at our Culver City clinic, we'll assess your tattoo's age, colors, and depth to provide a more accurate timeline. Some clients see significant fading after 3-4 sessions, but complete removal usually takes more treatments. We use the advanced PicoSure laser system, which often requires fewer sessions than older technologies.
Does tattoo removal hurt more than getting the tattoo?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to getting snapped with a rubber band - uncomfortable but manageable. We use a combination of topical numbing cream (applied 30 minutes before treatment) and a cooling system during the procedure to minimize discomfort. Each session only takes 5-15 minutes depending on the tattoo size. Many clients find removal less painful than the original tattooing process since our sessions are much shorter and we use advanced numbing methods.
