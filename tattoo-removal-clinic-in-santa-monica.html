Frequently Asked Questions About Tattoo Removal Clinic

How much does tattoo removal cost at your Santa Monica clinic?
The cost varies based on your tattoo's size, color, and density. At Think Again, small tattoos (up to 2x2 inches) typically start at $200 per session, medium tattoos (up to 4x4 inches) range from $300-400, and larger pieces are priced during consultation. We offer package pricing with 15% savings when you pre-pay for 6 sessions. We also provide free consultations where we can give you an exact quote after examining your tattoo in person.
How many sessions will I need to completely remove my tattoo?
Most clients need 6-12 sessions for complete removal, with treatments spaced 8-10 weeks apart. Black and dark blue tattoos typically require fewer sessions than colorful ones. During your consultation at our Santa Monica clinic, we'll assess your tattoo's age, colors, and depth to provide a more accurate timeline. We use the advanced PicoSure laser system, which often achieves results in fewer sessions compared to traditional Q-switched lasers.
Does tattoo removal hurt? What kind of pain management do you offer?
Most clients describe the sensation as similar to having a rubber band snapped against their skin. We use a combination of topical numbing cream (applied 30 minutes before treatment) and a cooling system during the procedure to minimize discomfort. Our PicoSure laser also causes less trauma to surrounding skin compared to older technologies. For especially sensitive areas, we can provide additional pain management options discussed during your consultation.
Are your laser technicians licensed and certified?
Yes, all our technicians are licensed by the State of California and have completed advanced certification in PicoSure laser technology. Our lead technician has over 8 years of experience in laser tattoo removal and holds additional certifications in skin care and laser safety. We maintain all required Los Angeles County health permits and undergo regular safety inspections.
What if my tattoo doesn't fade as expected? Do you offer any guarantees?
While complete removal can't be guaranteed due to factors like ink type and skin tone, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't see noticeable fading after 3 sessions following our aftercare instructions, we'll provide an additional session free of charge. We document your progress with high-resolution photos at each visit to track fading. If we determine during your consultation that your tattoo isn't a good candidate for removal, we'll tell you upfront rather than waste your time and money.
